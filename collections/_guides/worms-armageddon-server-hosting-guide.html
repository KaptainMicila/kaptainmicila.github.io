INTRODUCTION:

Some friends of mine, after playing Worms Armageddon together, wanted to play Worms by themself, and wanted to know how to host a game server outside of their LANs homes.

Did I seriously create a whole site just to write to your friends how to host a Worms game?

Yeah. Making a site it's not hard at all.

PART 1: THE IP ADDRESS

THE EASY WAY

Just open one of the many services available on the internet (just google it or something), and search for your IPv4 address!

It's suggested to NOT share this address with anyone.

But you're playing with people you probably trust, so it's ok.

THE HARD WAY

As the way it's not the same for all routers I'll not be writing it.

You'll have to check how to get your external IPv4 address from your router's model by yourself.

PART 3: PORT FORWARDING

You're lucky, Worms Armageddon has UPNP, (that means it's automatic).

If you want (or have to) do it manually, it's, sadly, another thing that you will have to search for yourself (thanks, router makers).

PART 4: HOSTING THE GAME

  1. Click the bottom left box
  2. Input your name in the Player Information area
  3. Click the top left box
  4. In the bottom right box, input the server's name and password
  5. Click Host
  6. ???
  7. Profit.

PART EXTRA: JOINING THE GAME

If you're in the same network

In step 4's window, you'll be able to see your friend's server in the top left box

If you're in different networks

In step 1-2's window, you'll have to type your friend's external IP in the bottom input form
